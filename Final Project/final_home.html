Welcome to my PAD evo helper. Puzzle and Dragons is a game created by Gung Ho Entertainment where the user collects monsters and uses the monster to fight through dugeons by matching orbs. These monsters need to be evolved to the "best" form to be able to beat the harder dungeons later in the game. This will only be helpful for 5 monsters. These monsters are Awoken Ra, Awonken Anubis, Awoken Izanagi, Awoken Horus, and Awoken Ares.
